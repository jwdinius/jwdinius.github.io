\resheading{Summary Statement}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\indent I am an experienced systems and software engineer looking for new opportunities in the field of autonomous systems.  I am a fast learner, self-starting, and I have completed projects in sensing and estimation, path planning, localization, and computer vision in autonomous vehicles.  I enjoy finding novel techniques to reduce complexity and improve system design.  I focus on clear documentation and clean user interfaces.
